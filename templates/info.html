Help & Information

Everything you need to know about K-FOOD LENS and how to use it effectively.

Allergy Information

Common allergens found in Korean cuisine that you should be aware of:

Gluten

A protein contained in wheat, barley, rye, etc. It is contained in flour products, noodles, dumpling skin, etc.

Soybean

Used as the main raw material for traditional Korean seasonings such as soy sauce, soybean paste, and red pepper paste.

Dairy Products

Dairy-related allergies such as milk, cheese, and butter. Used in modern Korean fusion dishes.

Nuts

Nut-related allergies such as peanuts, walnuts, and almonds. Often used in some Korean snacks and desserts.

Seafood

Various seafood including fish, shellfish, and crustaceans. Korean cuisine uses many seafood ingredients.

Eggs

Found in many Korean dishes including gimbap, bibimbap, and some stews and soups as garnish.

Vegetarian Suitability

Our app classifies Korean dishes into three vegetarian categories:

Completely Vegetarian

Dishes made entirely with plant-based ingredients. No animal products used in preparation.

Examples: Bibimbap (without meat), Japchae (vegetable version), Kimchi (vegan version)
Partially Vegetarian

Dishes that can be made vegetarian by removing or substituting certain ingredients.

Examples: Sundubu-jjigae (can omit seafood), Bibimbap (can omit meat), Kimbap (can customize)
Non-Vegetarian

Dishes with essential animal ingredients that cannot be modified to be vegetarian.

Examples: Samgyeopsal, Galbi, Most Korean soups and stews with meat/seafood base

How to Use the App

1

Take a Photo

On the 'Food Recognition' tab, press the 'Take or choose a food photo' button to upload an image of Korean food.

2

Analyze the Food

Press the 'Food Recognition' button to start artificial intelligence analysis of your Korean food.

3

View Results

Review the analysis results showing food information, allergen ingredients, and vegetarian suitability.

1

Capture Menu

On the 'Menu Recognition' tab, press the 'Take or select a menu photo' button to upload a picture of a Korean menu.

2

Translate

Press the 'Menu Board Text Recognition and Translation' button to process the Korean text and translate it to English.

3

Review Translation

Review both the original Korean text and English translation results to help you order with confidence.

1

Access History

Navigate to the 'Record' tab to see all your previously recognized foods and translated menus.

2

Filter Results

Use the filter options to show only food recognitions or menu translations if needed.

3

Review Details

Click on any history item to view the full details of your past recognitions and translations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the food recognition?

K-FOOD LENS can recognize more than 25 types of Korean foods with high accuracy (over 90%). The system is trained on thousands of food images and is continually improving.

Does the app work offline?

No, K-FOOD LENS requires an internet connection to analyze images and translate menus as it uses cloud-based AI services for optimal accuracy.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. Images you upload are used only for recognition and are not shared with third parties. You can delete your history at any time from your account settings.

What languages are supported for menu translation?

Currently, the app translates from Korean to English. We plan to add more languages in future updates based on user feedback and demand.

Can I suggest a new feature?

Absolutely! We welcome your feedback and suggestions. Please use the \"Contact\" option in the footer menu to send us your ideas.
